# Forge

> Building products that improve confidence in digital markets.

Forge is building the next generation of digital market infrastructure.

We believe better digital markets emerge from better systems—not better marketing.

Our first product focuses on improving how projects launch, evolve, and communicate with participants. Over time, Forge will expand into a broader suite of products designed to improve confidence, transparency, and decision-making across digital markets.

## Current Status

🚧 Active Development

Forge is currently in active development and being built in public.
