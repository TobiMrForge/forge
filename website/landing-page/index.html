How Forge is different

Enforced by contract.
Not promised by a team.

Every protection on Forge is a smart contract constraint, not a policy you're asked to trust.

On-chain enforcement
Contract-Enforced Locks

LP and creator allocations are locked by contract until $500K volume and 24 consecutive hours above launch price. No team override. No exceptions. The contract controls it, not us.

Sybil detection
Wallet Clustering Detection

Funding-source graph analysis identifies coordinated multi-wallet attacks before a token goes live. Bubble-map visualisations show every holder relationship clearly. Nothing hidden.

Trust algorithm
Trust-Weighted Discovery

Five signals: wallet diversity, holder retention, creator history, social verification, and volume-to-holder ratio surface legitimate projects. Gaming volume doesn't win here.

Graduation standards
100 Unique Wallets Required

Tokens must reach 100 genuinely unique holders before graduation. No synthetic volume, no concentrated supply clusters. Real community, or you don't graduate. The bar is the point.
